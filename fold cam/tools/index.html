Tools and Grippers

The TruBendCenter machines have a sophisticated set of tooling which enables you to form complex sheet metal parts.

Standard Tooling

The standard tools include:

  • Upper blank holder system

    • Standard blank holder

    • Left and right horn blank holder

    • Pick & Place blank holder

  • Lower blank holder

  • Upper/lower bending tool

The profiles of these tools are usually adapted to meet customer requirements, therefore they may vary accordingly in their dimensions.

Upper Blank Holder

The blank holder consists of a series of tools whose dimensions have been selected in such a way that, by combining them, all lengths of bending edges can be formed with a division of 5 mm. The combination can be calculated by the program during the creation of the program.

The tools used include:

Pick & Place Blank Holder

Up to six tools can be used, each of which is marked by a number. These always remain in the machine and can be used as needed. The tools are numbered from 1 to 4.

Standard Blank holder

These are tools which all have the same length. They always remain in the machine and can only be pushed to the right or left with the ToolMaster Bend.

Horn Blank Holder

These are tools that consist of one fixed part and one movable horn tool. The movable horn tool can be pulled in by the ToolMaster Bend so that a sheet can be inserted between two sides that have been bent.

Lower Blank Holder

The lower blank holder is a fixed tool that is mounted at the machine frame below the blank holder. This tool and the blank holder clamp the sheet. The lower blank holder bar is mounted on the lower blank holder by the operator. The lower blank holder makes it possible to bend folds with the rear side of the blank holder or with the extra blank holder tool abbreviated as ENW. During this, the horizontal forward movement of the part manipulator is limited.

Bending Tools

The bending tools are mounted on the blade holder. The profiles of these tools can be adapted to meet customer requirements, therefore they may vary accordingly in their dimensions..

ZBW Blades and ENW tools (Optional Tooling)

The machine can be fitted with optional equipment to enable complex bending tasks and to further increase the performance of the system.

Extra Blank Holder Tool (ENW)

The extra blank holder tool abbreviated as ENW is a fixture with which additional tools can be guided under the blank holder to be able to perform bends which would not be possible with the standard tool (such as narrow profiles or a negative bend after two short successive positive bends, etc.).

The extra blank holder tool is moved from the initial position by an NC electric motor vertically downwards.

Extra Bending Tool (ZBW)

The extra bending tool abbreviated as ZBW are used if special bends and/or partial bends have to be made that cannot be done with the standard bending tools. The extra bending tools are mounted on the carrier plate of the ZBW axis. The upper and lower inserted extra bending tools can be used independently of each other and lie on the bending tools during their use.
