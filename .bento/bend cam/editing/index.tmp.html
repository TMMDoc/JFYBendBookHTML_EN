After a bending solution is computed, there are several editing panels you can use to edit [1] the settings of a bend operation, the punch and die stations, the gauging or the angle-sensor configuration. Most of these panels are accessed simply by clicking on the appropriate element in the 3D display - for example, to edit the die station settings, just click on the die mount.
