• Switch to 2D mode and click on the measure icon.

  • To display the outer dimension, hover the mouse pointer over the bending line.

  • Click the bending line to specify the first measuring position, then place the mouse on a straight line/object (ON means that the 2nd position lies on straight line). The actual dimension is displayed.

  • Changing the correction factor 'k' leads to a change of the outer dimension. The correction factor k can only accept values from 0.1 to 0.9 mm.
