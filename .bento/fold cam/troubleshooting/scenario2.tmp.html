It is evident that the blank holder composition is too large. Two horn tools set up in the center have a bending length of 404 mm, which is obviously too much. The solution would be to use only one horn tool or to remove both, as they are not required.

  • Click on the collision symbol in the Bend navigator to go directly to the collision.

  • Click on the blank holder, select Horns as None and adjust the blank holder length so that no collision is displayed.
