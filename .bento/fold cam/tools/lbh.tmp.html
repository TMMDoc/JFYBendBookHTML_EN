The lower blank holder is only available in administrator mode.

Double-click on the relevant lower blank holder to activate or deactivate it for processing.

LBH-000 is supplied as the standard tool for the B34, and LBH-B37 as the standard tool for the B37.

The lower blank holder is a fixed tool that is mounted at the machine frame below the blank holder. This tool and the blank holder clamp the sheet. The lower blank holder makes it possible to bend folds with the rear side of the blank holder or with the extra blank holder tool (ENW). During this, the horizontal forward movement of the part manipulator is limited.
